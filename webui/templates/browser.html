Export Site Cookies

Log in to a site in the virtual browser, then export its cookies so CyberSeed can download members-only content.

  1. Open the virtual browser above
  2. Log in to the site normally
  3. Come back here, enter the domain and click Export
  4. Paste the site URL into the downloader as usual
